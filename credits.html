The statutes available on this platform have been digitized using the edition Statuti di Ascoli Piceno dell’anno MCCCLXXVII, edited by L. Zdekauer and P. Sella, Roma, Istituto storico italiano, 1910. This edition faithfully reproduces the text in the vernacular contained in the incunabulum of 1496, currently preserved at the Biblioteca del Senato in Rome. The images of the statutes have been provided, exclusively for the use of this website, by the Biblioteca del Senato "Giovanni Spadolini".
